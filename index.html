I am a postdoctoral associate at MIT CSAIL, adivsed by Prof. Antonio Torralba. I completed my PhD at Tel Aviv University, advised by Daniel Cohen-Or and Ariel Shamir.
My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision/graphics and art.
I focus on generative models within the context of design, creativity, and visual communication. I am particularly interested in exploring the hidden representations of large vision-language models and how their priors can be leveraged to enhance the way humans and computers communicate and convey meaning through visual elements.
