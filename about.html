Hi, I'm Yael

I am a PhD student at Tel Aviv University, advised by Prof. Daniel Cohen-Or and Prof. Ariel Shamir. I am currently also a student researcher at Google Research, in the Creative Camera team.

I live in Tel Aviv, and married to Assaf. I am interested in arts, and in my free time I like to draw and sculpt.

My research interests are Computer Graphics, Image Processing, and Machine Learning. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science from the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. During my B.Sc. I also studied Visual Communication at Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design. I was an intern at EPFL (VILAB), supervised by Prof. Amir Zamir. Prior to that I was a research intern at Disney Research, Zurich. My recent research work on sketch abstraction called CLIPasso won a best paper award in SIGGRAPH 2022.
